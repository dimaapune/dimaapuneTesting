About Our Academy

Discover our rich history, philosophy, and commitment to excellence in martial arts education

Our Story

DAI-ICHI Martial Arts Academy India (DIMAA) was founded in 1992 by Sensei K.B. Pawar (IV DAN: Yondan Black Belt) in Pune, Maharashtra. With a vision to spread mental, physical, and spiritual fitness through the discipline of karate, DIMAA has grown into one of the most respected institutions in traditional martial arts in India.

With over 33 years of experience, our academy has empowered thousands of students, many of whom have gone on to win district, state, and national-level championships. DIMAA stands for discipline, respect, and the pursuit of excellence through authentic karate training and self-defense education.

Our Philosophy

We believe that karate is a way of life. At DIMAA, we focus not only on physical techniques but also on character development, emotional strength, and spiritual growth. Our teaching is grounded in the principles of discipline, humility, perseverance, and self-respect.

We cultivate an inclusive and empowering atmosphere for learners of all ages, where every student is encouraged to grow at their own pace and reach their highest potential‚Äîboth inside and outside the dojo.

Our History

1992

Foundation

Dai-Ichi Martial Arts Academy was established in Pune by Sensei K.B. Pawar with the goal of promoting traditional karate and self-defense training in India.

2000

Milestone Achievements

Trained over 5000 students, with participants as young as 3 years old. Hosted inter-school and district-level karate championships.

2010

Black Belt Success

Produced 26 Black Belts including 7 Ni-Dan (2nd Degree), 6 Shodan (1st Degree), and 13 standard Black Belts.

2015

Corporate Outreach

Conducted self-defense awareness workshops for leading companies including Bajaj Allianz, Westin Hotel, and International Automotive Components.

2020

Global Affiliation

Became the India Head branch for JAPAN KARATE-DO RYOBU-KAI (JKR International), which has a presence in 20 countries worldwide.

2025

Legacy of Excellence

Over three decades of service in traditional martial arts, corporate training, and international certification with a commitment to discipline and community growth.
