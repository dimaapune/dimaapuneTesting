Meet Our Team

Dedicated instructors committed to your martial arts journey and personal growth

Our Expert Instructors

Our team consists of highly qualified martial arts instructors with decades of combined experience. Each instructor brings unique skills, perspectives, and specialties to create a comprehensive learning environment. We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of instruction while fostering personal growth and technical excellence in every student.

Sensei K. B. Pawar

Founder & Chief Instructor

4th DAN Black Belt (Goju-Ryu - Tenshi)

Sensei K. B. Pawar began his martial arts journey in 1982 and founded Dai Ichi Martial Arts Academy India with a mission to promote the art of karate across all age groups. With decades of experience and advanced martial arts training, he has played a pivotal role in spreading traditional karate across Pune and beyond. His leadership and dedication have helped train numerous instructors and popularize karate through disciplined and structured teaching methods.

Experience & Certifications
Black Belt – Indian Karate-Do-Kyo-Kushin-Kai Shodan – BINO-RYU Nidan – BINO-RYU Sandan – Goju-Ryu (Tenshi) Yondan – Goju-Ryu (Tenshi) JUDO – Poona JUDO Association Instructor – Asian Martial Arts Federation
Notable Achievements
  • Referee – Poona District Martial Arts Tournament (Cobra Martial Arts Indian Association, 1991)
  • Jury – Poona District Martial Arts Tournament (Cobra Martial Arts Indian Association, 1992)
  • Referee – 2nd Pune District Level Tournament (Shikijima Martial Arts Academy, 1995)
  • Judge – 2nd Pune District Level Tournament (Shikijima Martial Arts Academy, 1995)
